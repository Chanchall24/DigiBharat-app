ID Document Upload Guide

Do's
  • Use clear, high-resolution images
  • Ensure good lighting
  • Keep document flat and straight
  • Include all corners of the document
  • Use supported formats (JPG, PNG, PDF)
Don'ts
  • Don't use blurry or out-of-focus images
  • Avoid shadows or glare
  • Don't crop important information
  • Don't use damaged documents
  • Don't exceed 16MB file size
Pro Tip

For best results, take the photo during daytime near a window for natural lighting, or use a well-lit room with white background.
